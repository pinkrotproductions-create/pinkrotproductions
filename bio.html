what the spud is PINK ROT PRODUCTIONS???

pink rot etymology

pink rot is a potato disease that manifests itself as pink discolouration.

"Pink Rot - Quick Facts." Crop IPM, https://cropipm.omafra.gov.on.ca/en-ca/crops/potatoes/diseases/pinkrot. Accessed 13 March, 2026.

pink rot mission

the pink rot mission is to help artists help each other succeed by connecting them with as many members of the community as possible.

pink rot values

pink rot values are inclusion, individuality, community, and humanity. no artist is left behind. we uplift each other to be the best version we can be.

pink rot purpose

the pink rot purpose is to support independant local creators by providing affordable networking and event curation services.

pink rot story

everyone enjoys potatoes. pink rot productions noticed a lack of support for local artists, and felt the need to bring focus back to local communities instead of feeding the endless, all-consuming black hole that is hollywood, and the rich.

pink rot policy and procedures

pink rot policy does not condone hate or harrassment of any sort, and will not work with persons who do not abide by the policy.

pink rot terms and conditions

there is space for everyone to share their art. express your individuality, find your people, use your energy to uplift others instead of for negativity.
